Some image formats that can be used:

JPGs

JPG images are good for photos.

GIFs

GIFS are good for animations.

PNGs

PNGs are good for diagrams and icons.

SVGs

SVGs are amazing. Use them whenever you can. They can be resized without losing quality.

A problem with them: they have a large file size when they have a lot of text.
